What happens after submission

A simple review process without automatic promises.

Every inquiry is different. These steps describe the general review process rather than a guaranteed timeline or outcome.

  1. Review

    The inquiry details are reviewed

    The audience, requested topic, format and general context are considered.

  2. Clarify

    Additional context may be requested

    A reply may ask about team size, workplace needs, preferred timing or delivery expectations.

  3. Discuss options

    Relevant next steps may be discussed

    Where appropriate and available, possible resources, formats or independent specialist connections may be considered.

Submitting an inquiry does not create a service agreement, confirm availability or guarantee a training provider, schedule, price or outcome.

Contact and inquiry FAQ

Before you send a message.

Review the most common questions about training inquiries, specialist availability and sensitive information.

Explore Training Topics
Does SecureHabit provide every training session directly?

No. SecureHabit provides educational information and an inquiry platform. Some inquiries may be reviewed for possible connection with independent training or cybersecurity specialists where appropriate and available.

What information should I include in a training inquiry?

Include the approximate audience size, relevant awareness topics, preferred learning format, workplace context and general timing. Do not include sensitive operational or personal information.

Can specialist availability be guaranteed?

No. Specialist availability, location, scope, timing, pricing and suitability depend on the individual inquiry and available independent providers.

Can I send confidential information or incident evidence?

No. Do not submit passwords, credentials, authentication codes, confidential documents, incident evidence, regulated personal data or other sensitive workplace material.

How are submitted details handled?

Submitted details are handled according to the information described in the Privacy Policy .
