About Us

Practical awareness is what we do.

SecureHabit helps organizations make workplace security awareness clearer, more practical and easier to connect with everyday decisions employees already make.

Instead of overwhelming teams with technical language, the focus stays on familiar workplace situations: messages, passwords, file sharing, remote work, approvals and responsible handling of information across normal business activity.

Phishing Review Checklist

A concise six-point review for unexpected messages, links, files and requests.

  1. Review the full sender address.
  2. Check the message context.
  3. Inspect links and attachments.
  4. Review the requested action.
  5. Verify independently.
  6. Follow the reporting process.

Password Safety Reminder

A short account-safety reminder covering unique credentials, managers, MFA and recovery.

  1. Use a unique approved credential.
  2. Follow password-manager guidance.
  3. Protect authentication prompts.
  4. Store recovery codes appropriately.
  5. Do not approve unexpected sign-ins.
  6. Use the approved recovery route.
